## 2. [4 points]

Express the following sentences in propositional logic using the propositions $p$ : "You can get the scholarship", $q$ : "You get an A on the final exam." and logical connectives.
1. You can get the scholarship only if you get an A on the final exam.

<Solution>
$$p \to q$$
</Solution>

2. You can get the scholarship unless you didn't get an A on the final exam.

<Solution>
$$q \to p$$
</Solution>
