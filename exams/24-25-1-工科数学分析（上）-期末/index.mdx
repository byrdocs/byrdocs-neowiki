## 四 计算积分 (每小题6分, 共12分)

### 1.

$\int\frac{\mathrm dx}{x+\sqrt{1-x^2}}$.

<Solution>
令 $x=\sin t$，则原积分转化为

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\int\frac{\cos t\,\mathrm dt}{\sin t+\cos t}\\
=&\frac12\int\frac{\sin t+\cos t}{\sin t+\cos t}\,\mathrm dt+\frac12\int\frac{\cos t-\sin t}{\sin t+\cos t}\,\mathrm dt\\
=&\frac12\int\mathrm dt+\frac12\int\frac{\mathrm d(\sin t+\cos t)}{\sin t+\cos t}\\
=&\frac t2+\frac12\ln\left|\sin t+\cos t\right|+C
\end{aligned}
$$
</Solution>
